Baked Chicken Tacos

A classic Mexican recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups salsa chicken (Try my Slow Cooker Salsa Chicken with Black Beans, with excess liquid drained off)
  • 10-12 taco shells (see note)
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • Toppings such as lettuce, sour cream, sliced jalapeños, salsa, guacamole, cilantro, etc.

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F. Coat a 13×9-inch baking dish with cooking spray.
  2. Stand the taco shells up in the baking pan. Bake the empty shells for 5 minutes.
  3. Remove the pan from the oven and divide the chicken between the taco shells, then top with the cheese.
  4. Bake for another 5-7 minutes until the cheese is melted. Serve with your favorite toppings.
